## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need a commercial license for a side project?

If your side project generates revenue or is intended to generate revenue, yes. If it's purely for learning or personal use with no commercial intent, the MIT license applies.

### Can I use one license for multiple client projects?

- **Hobby**: No, one project only
- **Pro/Director**: Yes, unlimited projects

### What counts as a "commercial project"?

Any project that:

- Charges users money (subscriptions, one-time payments)
- Generates revenue through ads
- Is sold to clients
- Is used internally by a for-profit company
