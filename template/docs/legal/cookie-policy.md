## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function properly, provide a better experience, and give owners information about usage.

**By Duration:**

- **Session Cookies:** Expire when you close your browser
- **Persistent Cookies:** Remain for a set period or until deleted

**By Origin:**

- **First-Party Cookies:** Set by the website you are visiting
- **Third-Party Cookies:** Set by third-party services (analytics, payments)

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### Browser Settings

**Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data

**Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

**Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies

**Edge:** Settings → Cookies and site permissions

### Impact of Disabling Cookies

| Cookie Type | Impact if Disabled                              |
| ----------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
